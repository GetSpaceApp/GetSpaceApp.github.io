Privacy Policy

SPACE App · Effective July 1, 2025 · Developer: Jakkula Shiva Kumar · spaceapp.page@gmail.com

How SPACE collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

1. Introduction and Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how Jakkula Shiva Kumar ("we," "us," "our," or "Developer") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your personal information when you use the SPACE mobile application ("App") available on the Google Play Store. SPACE is a social habit-tracking app that lets users connect with groups who share similar habits and goals.

This Policy primarily applies to users in India and complies with the Information Technology Act, 2000, the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011 ("SPDI Rules"), and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 ("DPDPA 2023"). We also maintain voluntary compliance with international standards (GDPR, CCPA) for future global expansion.

2. Developer Identity (Data Fiduciary)

Under the DPDPA 2023, the "Data Fiduciary" responsible for your personal data is:

  • Name: Jakkula Shiva Kumar
  • Role: Individual Developer / Data Fiduciary
  • Country: India
  • Contact: spaceapp.page@gmail.com

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account Information (email address, display name); Profile Information (optional avatar, photo, bio, location); Habit Data (habit names, descriptions, emojis, schedules, completion logs); Social Interaction Data (messages, reactions, nudges, snap photos, and captions within shared spaces); User-Generated Content (photos you upload).

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

With your explicit consent: Location Data (coordinates for Discover features); Usage Data (habit completion timestamps, streak data); Device Information (push notification tokens only).

3.3 Sensitive Personal Data or Information (SPDI) Under Indian Law

Under the SPDI Rules, 2011, certain data qualifies as "Sensitive Personal Data." From the categories defined in the Rules, we collect: passwords (stored as hashed/encrypted values only) and location information (with your explicit consent). We do not collect financial information, health data, biometric data, or sexual orientation. Any SPDI is collected only with your free, informed, specific, and clear consent.

3.4 What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect your full legal name (unless used as display name), use third-party analytics SDKs, collect payment card details (handled entirely by Google Play Billing), or access device contacts, camera roll, or microphone beyond what you explicitly grant.

4. Consent and Legal Basis

Under DPDPA 2023 (India): We process your personal data based on your free, specific, informed, unconditional, and unambiguous consent ("consent" as defined under Section 6 of DPDPA 2023). You may withdraw consent at any time. Withdrawal of consent will not affect the lawfulness of processing prior to withdrawal but may result in reduced App functionality.

Under GDPR (EU/EEA — for future users): Performance of a Contract (Article 6(1)(b)) for core features; Legitimate Interests (Article 6(1)(f)) for security; Consent (Article 6(1)(a)) for location, photos, and push notifications; Legal Obligation (Article 6(1)(c)) for applicable laws.

Under CCPA/CPRA (California — for future users): We do not sell personal information. California residents may request disclosure of data collected and request deletion.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to: create and maintain your account; provide core App features (habit tracking, streaks, couple/group spaces, challenges, leaderboards, progress calendars); provide location-based Discover features; display your profile to members of shared spaces; deliver push notifications; enforce our Terms of Service; maintain App security; and comply with legal obligations under Indian law. We do not use your data for advertising or automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

6. Data Storage and Security

6.1 Where Your Data is Stored

Your data is stored on servers operated by Supabase, Inc. in the South Asia (Mumbai, India) region. Supabase is ISO 27001 certified. Profile photos and habit snaps are stored in Supabase Storage.

Note for Indian users: Your data is stored within India, in compliance with applicable data localisation expectations under the DPDPA 2023 and IT Act 2000. We will continue to align with any further data localisation requirements as notified by the Central Government.

6.2 Security Measures

As required under the SPDI Rules, 2011, we maintain reasonable security practices including: all data in transit is encrypted using TLS; all data at rest is encrypted by Supabase's infrastructure; access is controlled by Row Level Security (RLS) policies; authentication uses JWT tokens via Supabase Auth; habit snap photos automatically expire and are deleted daily.

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We share data only with:

  • Supabase, Inc. — backend infrastructure (data processor)
  • Google LLC — payment confirmation only, via Google Play Billing
  • Legal authorities — if required by Indian law, court order, or government direction under the IT Act 2000 or DPDPA 2023
  • Business transfer — in a merger or acquisition, with prior notification to you

Any third-party data processors are contractually obligated to handle your data with equivalent protection standards.

8. Location Data

Your location coordinates are used exclusively for proximity-based recommendations in the Discover section. They are never used for advertising or sold to third parties. Location collection requires your explicit consent via your device's permission system. You may revoke this permission at any time via your device settings, after which location-based features will be unavailable.

9. User-Generated Content

Snaps are visible to all members of the shared space in which they are posted. Snaps automatically expire at midnight IST daily and are permanently deleted from our servers. Your display name, avatar, and habit activity are visible to other members of spaces you join.

10. Data Retention

Account data: retained until account deletion. Habit snaps: deleted daily at midnight IST. Profile photographs: deleted upon replacement or account deletion. Push notification tokens: retained until permission is revoked or account deleted. Legal/financial records: up to 7 years where required by Indian law (e.g., IT Act, GST records if applicable).

11. Your Rights as a Data Principal (DPDPA 2023)

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the following rights as a "Data Principal":

  • Right to Access (Section 11): Obtain a summary of personal data we hold about you and the processing activities undertaken.
  • Right to Correction and Erasure (Section 12): Correct inaccurate or incomplete data; erase data no longer necessary for the purpose it was collected.
  • Right to Grievance Redressal (Section 13): Have grievances addressed within a reasonable timeframe. Contact us at spaceapp.page@gmail.com.
  • Right to Nominate (Section 14): Nominate another individual to exercise your rights in the event of your death or incapacity.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time, without affecting prior lawful processing.

EU/EEA users additionally retain rights to data portability and lodging complaints with their local Data Protection Authority. California users retain CCPA/CPRA rights.

To exercise any right, contact: spaceapp.page@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days as required under DPDPA 2023.

12. Grievance Officer (IT Act 2000 / SPDI Rules)

As required under Rule 5(9) of the SPDI Rules, 2011, we have designated a Grievance Officer to address complaints regarding the processing of your personal data:

  • Name: Jakkula Shiva Kumar
  • Email: spaceapp.page@gmail.com
  • Response Time: Within 30 days of receipt of complaint

13. Account Deletion

You may delete your account via the App (Settings > Account > Delete Account) or by emailing spaceapp.page@gmail.com. We will permanently delete your personal data within 30 days. Certain records may be retained for up to 7 years where required by Indian law. Account deletion is irreversible.

14. Children's Privacy

SPACE is intended for users aged 18 and older. Under the DPDPA 2023, processing of personal data of children (under 18) requires verifiable parental consent. We do not knowingly collect data from minors under 18 without such consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided data without consent, contact us at spaceapp.page@gmail.com and we will delete the data promptly.

Note: If you intend to allow users aged 13–17, you must implement a verifiable parental consent mechanism as required under Section 9 of the DPDPA 2023 before doing so.

15. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored within India (Supabase's Mumbai data centre). No international transfer of your personal data currently takes place. If future infrastructure changes require cross-border transfers, we will ensure compliance with any restricted country notifications under DPDPA 2023. For future EU/EEA users, transfers will be made in accordance with GDPR requirements including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) where applicable.
