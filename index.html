connection.

Not another app you'll forget about. SPACE makes habits stick through real people, real proof, and real community.

How It Works

Your journey
in four steps.

Step 01
Create Your SPACE
Sign up in seconds. Set up your first habits — morning routines, workouts, reading, hydration — whatever matters to you. Your solo space is free, forever.
Step 02
Build Your Streak
Check off habits daily. Watch your streak counter grow. Share snaps of your progress. The calendar fills up and your consistency becomes undeniable.
Step 03
Find Your Crew
Invite your partner for a Couple Space or rally your squad for a Group Space. Combined streaks, shared challenges, and daily snaps keep everyone accountable.
Step 04
Discover Your Tribe
Open the Discover tab and find real communities near you — running crews, reading clubs, meditation groups. Your people are a block away. Go find your aura.
